# Datasheet: NHANES reference extract

Supplied files: `RHQ_L.xpt`, `DEMO_L.xpt`, and `BMX_L.xpt` from NHANES 2021–2023. They are used only to produce a survey-weighted reference distribution. No individual NHANES record is used as a model-training label or linked to the structured dataset. `WTINT2YR` is required for population-rate calculations.
