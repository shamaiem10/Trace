04 / transparency built in

Trace is designed to leave researchers with reusable records—not unsupported certainty.

◌Research-only by design

Trajectory summaries never diagnose, recommend treatment, or claim clinical validation.

↗Missingness stays visible

Blank measures are reported as missing rather than silently imputed or hidden.
